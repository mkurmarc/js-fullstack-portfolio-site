About Me

Iwas born and raised. On a magical rock called Inishbofin Island, I taught myself to design and build websites. These days I live in Galway City and work with the amazing team at Ex Ordo advancing conference software.

I hold an honours degree in Mathematics and Music Technology. I previously studied Electronic Engineering and Astrophysics. Designing & building websites became my passion in college so I kept at it 👨‍💻.

Personally, I love surfing and playing music, I am obsessed with my camper van 🚐 and I try to get away in it as much as possible. I am most happy in the water 🏄‍♂️.
